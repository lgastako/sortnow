Let There Be Sort!

Usage

  1. Drag this bookmarklet to your bookmarks bar: Sort Now
  2. Visit a page with unsortable tables.
  3. Click the bookmarklet.
  4. Enjoy sortable tables, usually

Disclaimer

This bookmarklet may impregnate your sister or cause other unexpected and/or undesirable side affects. It is provided without any warranty and all use is at your own risk.

Example

Feel free to try it on the table below:

First Last Rank Serial Number
Edward Disarray General 54-334-445
William Mustard Colonel 69-157-402
Alexander Havok Major 12-666-334

Note

While the tables are now sortable, no immediate visual change will take place. When you click a header to initiate a sort you will see an arrow appear by the currently sorted header indicating the direction of the sort.
